About Earth.MC

Earth.MC is a geopolitical Minecraft server featuring a 1:1000 scale Earth map. Players establish nations, manage economies, conduct diplomacy, and participate in strategic warfare across all continents.

Nations and Warfare

Establish or join nations, declare wars, and conquer territories through strategic military operations.

Real Earth Map

Navigate an accurate Earth terrain map featuring real-world geography, biomes, and landmasses.

Political Systems

Engage in complex diplomatic relations, negotiate treaties, and form international alliances.

Economic Development

Develop national economies through resource management, trade agreements, and infrastructure development.

World-Ending Events

Unite or Perish

When the world faces extinction, humanity must choose: work together to survive, or tear each other apart in the chaos. Catastrophic events will threaten to end the current season. Only through global cooperation can players extend their world's lifespan.

But beware. Not everyone wants to save the world. Some players will support the apocalypse, fighting for a fresh start. The server will be divided. Wars will erupt. And in the end, the fate of the world rests in the hands of those brave enough to fight for it.

Catastrophic Threats

Meteor strikes. Pandemic outbreaks. Climate collapse. Nuclear winter. Events that force global response or guarantee extinction.

Global Cooperation

Nations must unite, sharing resources and coordinating efforts to prevent apocalypse. Diplomacy becomes survival.

Internal Conflict

Factions emerge supporting the end. Saboteurs attack recovery efforts. Civil wars erupt amid global crisis. Chaos reigns.

These events are far in the future. For now, you can relax, build your nations, and enjoy the peace. But when the time comes, the world will need heroes.
